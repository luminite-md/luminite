# Luminite

Your notes are scattered across Bear, Notion, Feishu, Apple Notes — trapped in silos that don't talk to each other, locked behind APIs you don't control.

Luminite pulls them all back. Indexes everything locally. Lets AI understand your knowledge — on your machine, with nothing leaving your disk.

No cloud. No subscriptions. No "we updated our privacy policy" emails.

Just your second brain, running on your hardware, answering only to you.
